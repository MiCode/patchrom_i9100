Bluetooth tethering

You can tether your Android device to your computer via a Bluetooth connection, to share your device's Internet connection with your computer
  • Bluetooth tethering works with any computer or other device that can obtain an Internet connection via Bluetooth

  • For more information, see http://www.android.com/tether#bluetooth
